Our Story

Bringing Indian Elegance to Floral Design

Swan was founded in Mumbai by Priya Sharma with a vision to blend traditional Indian floral aesthetics with contemporary design. What began as a small boutique has grown into India's premier luxury floral design studio.

Our team of master florists combines traditional Indian techniques with innovative approaches to create arrangements that honor cultural heritage while embracing modern elegance. We source the finest local and international blooms to ensure every creation meets our exacting standards.

Each arrangement is crafted with meticulous attention to detail, reflecting our philosophy that true luxury lies in the perfect harmony of tradition and innovation.
